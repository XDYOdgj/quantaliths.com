Stylish & Energy-Efficient Under Cabinet Lighting

The Jesco Lighting SG5-14/41 is a sleek, modern under-cabinet light fixture designed to blend effortlessly into your kitchen, bathroom, or workspace. With its 14-watt T5 fluorescent lamps, it offers superior energy efficiency and a long lifespan of up to 20,000 hours, reducing maintenance and electricity costs over time.

The white enamel finish ensures a timeless look that complements any interior style, while the durable ribbed acrylic diffuser provides even light distribution and prevents unwanted glare. The built-in instant-start ballast delivers a smooth, flicker-free light output and operates quietly, making it perfect for residential and commercial environments alike.

This fixture is built with flexibility in mind. It features a modular design that allows you to link up to 10 units together, creating customizable lighting setups that match your specific requirements. Easy installation is a key benefit, thanks to its direct connectors and mounting clips, ideal for both DIY projects and professional installations.

With a cool 4100K color temperature, this under-cabinet light provides a natural daylight effect, making your space appear brighter and more welcoming. Whether you're illuminating a kitchen island or a dedicated workspace, the SG5-14/41 combines form and function to elevate your lighting experience.
