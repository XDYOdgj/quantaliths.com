Peugeot Bistro Pepper and Salt Mill Set

The Peugeot Bistro Pepper and Salt Mill Set is a stylish and functional kitchen essential that adds a touch of elegance and tradition to your meal preparation. Inspired by the iconic Peugeot mills from 1874, this set is designed to reflect the refinement of French cafés and bring a timeless appeal to your countertop. With its sleek, balanced form, it fits seamlessly into both modern and classic kitchen setups, making it a versatile addition for any food enthusiast.

Each mill is crafted with precision, ensuring it's engineered specifically for its respective spice. The adjustable grinding knob allows you to customize the texture of your seasoning, whether you're looking for a fine, sharp grind for a delicate dish or a coarser texture for a bold flavor. The two-stage burr mechanism first cracks the peppercorns and then grinds them to your preferred consistency, unlocking the full depth of flavor and aroma in every use.

Made from sustainably sourced beechwood, these mills are not only eco-friendly but also durable, with a water-based finish that maintains their natural beauty and smooth handling. The set features a lightweight and compact design that's easy to store and simple to use, combining tradition with modern convenience. Whether you're seasoning a steak, adding a dash to your soup, or enhancing popcorn, this set delivers unmatched quality and performance.
